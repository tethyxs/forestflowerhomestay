The Estate

Nestled in the heart of Coorg, famously known as the Scotland of India, The Estate is surrounded by rolling hills, dense forests, sprawling coffee plantations, and the sacred Cauvery River. Located amidst the breathtaking Western Ghats, this tranquil retreat captures the essence of Coorg's natural beauty and charm. With misty mornings, scenic landscapes, and serene surroundings, it offers the perfect escape for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and those looking to unwind in comfort.
